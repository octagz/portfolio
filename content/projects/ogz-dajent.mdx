# The Adobe Suite for DJs

DaJent unleashes the creative potential of DJs by turning the overwhelming process of building a set into one connected workflow, saving them tens of hours monthly on prep and giving them back the time to refine their own style.

Behind every two-hour DJ set is hours of preparation most people never see. DJs discover music across platforms like Spotify, SoundCloud, and Instagram, then manually download, tag, and organize each track before it can be used in a performance. BPM, key, energy level, genre, and cue points must all be set by hand across libraries that can grow to thousands of tracks. Semi-professional DJs spend 7 to 15 hours per week managing their music libraries instead of practicing or refining their sets.

DaJent was created to eliminate this hidden labor. The name stands for "da agent": an AI assistant that knows each DJ personally and helps them materialize their creative vision.

# The Journey

### Customer Discovery

We started by talking to DJs. Over 4 months we conducted 40+ interviews across different genres, experience levels, and geographies to understand how DJs actually work — where the pain is, what tools they use, and what they wish existed. These conversations shaped every product decision.

### Identifying the Workflow Gap

The interviews revealed that the core problem isn't any single tool — it's the disconnection between them. DJs juggle separate apps for discovery, purchasing, tagging, and performance prep. No single product connects the full journey from finding a track to playing it live. That gap became our focus.

### Designing the Solution

DaJent connects the entire DJ workflow in four steps:

1. **Discover** — Aggregate music discoveries from Spotify, SoundCloud, YouTube, and Instagram into one place
2. **Acquire** — Move from discovery to purchase in one flow
3. **Curate** — AI analyzes each track and assigns BPM, key, energy level, and custom tags based on the DJ's personal preferences. DJs approve or adjust, and the system learns over time
4. **Export** — Build sets and export performance-ready to USB

A key design principle: DJs care deeply about creative autonomy. They'll adopt AI only if it personalizes to them rather than replacing their judgment. So DaJent follows a human-in-the-loop approach — assistance without reduction of autonomy.

# Building the Business

### Market Validation

- 40+ primary interviews over 4 months (Dec 2025 – Mar 2026)

# Origin Story

As a software engineer learning to DJ, I quickly noticed how outdated and fragmented existing DJ tools are. After hours of organizing and tagging, a DJ's creative energy is spent before the performance even begins. The most rewarding part of DJing is performing: reading the crowd, building energy, watching the dance floor respond. DaJent was created so DJs can spend less time managing their libraries and more time doing what they love.
