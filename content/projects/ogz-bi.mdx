In this initiative, a comprehensive business intelligence application was built for materials manufacturers. It integrated various technologies such as React for frontend development, Lambda functions, Athena, and DynamoDB for backend services, and Airflow for orchestrating complex data pipelines. This application played a crucial role in streamlining data management and enhancing decision-making processes.
